Hi there! This is the website of Greg Daynes, and is where I collect my notes, write about projects I’m working on and try a bit of self promotion.

I’m a full-stack software developer from Vancouver Island, Canada. I have been working in web development since 2003, and as a hobbyist since 1996. while the web is massive, and changes every day, I love to stay on top of new developments by creating prototypes and tests to find understanding for business, and in personal computing. You can find some of these experiments in Projects.

I love to discuss code, system architecture, design and learning new techniques & patterns. Please reach me with email@gregdaynes.com or on Bluesky @gregdaynes.
