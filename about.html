Hello! I’m Greg Daynes, a seasoned, salty Full-Stack Software Developer Consultant, and Fractional CTO based in British Columbia, Canada.

With two decades of experience, my professional adventure has been exciting through the dynamic technology of Print Media, Advertising, Telcom, Defense, Education and E-commerce.

Having played senior end executive roles in diverse domains, I’ve directed long-term roadmaps and contributed to the goal of sustainable (and maintainable) software solutions. Whether as an individual contributor or in my capacity as a Fractional CTO, my experiences bring a wealth of expertise to any development teams and product management.

I have a personal commitment to accessibility, performance, and efficiency. This forms my passion for guiding teams towards success, delivering tangible results that go beyond my role as being just a developer.
